My first holiday in Mahanoro

Was it optional to stay in Mahanoro during the holiday?

It had been my longing to meet up with all friends and visit my relatives during the last holiday but by means of this pandemic, I could not fulfill my desire because our town was still locked-down.

Was it a big problem for me?
No. Not a problem at all!. We had such a great week-holiday here. A litle bit problem is that everyone is not relly allowed to go out because of the confinement and it is not only here but wherever we go, it is the same.

How did I spend spend the holiday?

On the fist two days, I had a difficulty in passig the time. It was kind of longer than usual because I did not have a lot of activity to do apart from reading a book, being on line which became boring if not altered with other things.

We were riviving some of the malagasy games like Fanorona, katro, vitsly,... which is really captivating, and lerned some cool stuff as well such as photography,...
