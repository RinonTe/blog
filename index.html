Introduction

I am called Rinon, 21 years old. I was born and raised in Vodiriana which is one of the small towns inside of Moramanga's ditrict (Madagascar). At the moment, I am settling in Mahanoro which is a city and rural commune located in the Atsinanana region of eastern Madagascar, along the coast. I have been living here for 13 months in order to look for a different life and study as well.
